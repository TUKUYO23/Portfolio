HOW TO MAKE DELICIOUS SWEETS

Do you like and make sweets? I love making sweets. I'm good at make bolo and macaroons, so I will teach you how to make delicious Bolo.

BOLO

MATERIAL...Yolk(one) Potato starch(90g) sugar(30g) Milk(5g)Honey(10g)

1.Put in the yolk and sugar in a bowl, and mix.

2.Add the potato starch, and mix.

3.Add milk until it is firm enough to roll, and mix.

4.Shape in circle that about 1cm.

5.Bake in 180°　oven for 10min.
